Agulhas Current blog post

Last year I received the once in a lifetime opportunity to sail across the agulhas current with people from all around the world.

I was busy finishing up my diploma and saw the application for all students in marine research to sail agross the agulhas current on a research vessel free of charge. I was able to encounter text book knowledge in real-world setting. I've witnessed seals dissection, whale-watching and the works!
