Agulhas Current blog post

Last year I received a once in a lifetime opportunity to sail across the agulhas current with people from all around the world.

I was busy finishing up my diploma in Marine Science when I saw the application for all students, in marine research, to sail agross the agulhas current on a research vessel free of charge. I encountered text book knowledge in a real-world setting. I've witnessed seal dissections, whale-watching, CTD deployment and the works!
